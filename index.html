Featured Projects

A selection of technical and strategic projects spanning reservoir engineering, analytics, and sustainability innovation.

CHO Program Performance Dashboard

Cenovus Energy | 2025-2026

Developed a Power BI dashboard to replace manual PowerPoint lookbacks and create a reusable framework for evaluating Conventional Heavy Oil program performance over time.

The dashboard supported forecast versus actual performance review, economics, subsurface trends, and program-level comparisons, with a structure designed to scale year over year rather than function as a one-off analysis.

CHO Tracer Program Dashboard, Workflow & Comment App

Cenovus Energy | 2025-2026

Played a leading role in the CHO tracer program across 33 multilateral wells, supporting inflow allocation, tracer mass balance calculations, production interpretation, and cross-team technical communication.

Built and documented repeatable workflows, contributed to a Power BI dashboard for tracer analysis, and developed a Power Apps-based comment form embedded within the dashboard to improve structured collaboration and interpretation.

CHO Hole Size Analysis Dashboard

Cenovus Energy | 2025-2026

Developed a dashboard to evaluate the impact of production hole size on well performance, including both absolute and normalized production comparisons across well groups.

The work required extensive data cleaning, manual validation, well classification, and comparison design to support drilling and completion optimization decisions with a reusable analytical tool.

SiteLoop | Waste-to-Value Venture Concept

Avatar Innovations Ignite | 2026

Co-developed a venture concept focused on converting remote camp organic waste into value for reclamation, connecting waste management, carbon opportunity, and topsoil needs into a circular system.

The concept explored in-vessel composting for remote industrial camps, with the goal of reducing landfill trucking, lowering disposal costs, supporting reclamation, and creating shared value across operations, environmental, and reclamation teams.
